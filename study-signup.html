Help us find out if this actually works.

We built tools based on what the research says. Now we need to test whether they make a difference. That's what this study is for.

What you'd actually do

1
Take one assessment today — the Passive vs. Active Use assessment or the General Wellbeing assessment. Takes about 4-5 minutes. Done right here on the site, nothing stored anywhere.
2
Try the strategies for 3 weeks — based on your results, you'll get specific strategies for your pattern. Use them however works for you. No check-ins, no meetings.
3
Retake the same assessment — we'll reach out after 3 weeks with a link. Take the same assessment again. That's it.
4
Fill out a short feedback form — tell us what worked, what didn't, and what you'd change. Takes about 2 minutes. Your honest feedback matters more to us than positive feedback.

About your data: We collect your email or Discord handle only so we can send you the follow-up link in 3 weeks. Assessment responses are scored in your browser and never sent to us. The only data we see is your pre/post scores (not individual answers) and your feedback. We don't share anything with anyone.

Common questions

How much time does this take total?
About 15 minutes spread across 3 weeks — roughly 5 minutes for each assessment plus 2-3 minutes for the feedback form. No meetings, no check-ins, no ongoing commitment.
What data do you collect?
Just your name/handle (for follow-up), age range, and which assessment you took. Your actual assessment answers are scored in your browser and never sent to us. We see your overall scores at pre and post, and your feedback form — that's it.
Do I have to use the strategies?
We'd like you to try them, but honestly, we also want to know what happens when people don't. So just be honest when you fill out the feedback form either way.
What if I forget to do the follow-up?
We'll send you a reminder. If you still miss it, no problem — we won't chase you down. But the follow-up is the most important part for us, so we'd really appreciate it.
Is this an official research study?
Not yet. This is a pilot study to gather preliminary data before approaching university partners. We're being transparent about that because we think it matters.
