Content & Social Media

Social Media & Content Intern

Research

Research Intern

Best fit: someone who actually likes reading papers and wants research experience.
Outreach

Partnerships & Outreach Intern

Best fit: someone who's comfortable reaching out to people they don't know and can write a clear, professional email.
Engineering

Web & Product Intern

Help improve the website, build new interactive tools (like the sleep tracker we recently launched), and make the assessment experience better. HTML/CSS/JS is the minimum — React or Python experience is a plus.

Best fit: someone who can ship working code and cares about building something that actually helps people.
